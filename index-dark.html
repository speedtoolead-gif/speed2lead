The problem

Most leads call the first agent who responds

If that's not you, it's the agent down the street. Every minute you're slow is a listing someone else closes.

The 5-minute gap

Respond within 5 minutes and you're 21x more likely to qualify a lead. After 30 minutes, it drops to almost nothing. Most agents take hours.

You can't be everywhere

How it works

The AI qualifies the lead, asks about timeline and budget, and books them directly into your calendar.
