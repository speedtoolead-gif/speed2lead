Questions

How long does setup take?

About 2 days. We connect your WhatsApp, train the AI on your listings and speaking style, and you're live. Nothing for you to install or configure.

Do I need to change my CRM?

No. We connect to Airtable out of the box, or we can connect to your existing CRM through an API. No migration needed.

What if the AI can't answer a question?

It forwards the conversation to you right away. The AI knows its limits. It's good at qualifying and scheduling, but it won't try to negotiate a deal for you.

Is this compliant with real estate regulations?

Yes. Every opt-in and opt-out is logged. All conversations are stored. We built TCPA compliance into the system from the start, not as an afterthought.
