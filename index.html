FAQ

How long does setup take?

About 2 days. We connect your WhatsApp, train the AI on your listings and speaking style, and you're live. Nothing for you to install.

Do I need to change my CRM?

No. We connect to Airtable out of the box, or your existing CRM through an API. No migration needed.

What if the AI can't answer a question?

It forwards the conversation to you right away. The AI knows its limits — good at qualifying and scheduling, won't negotiate a deal for you.

Is this compliant with real estate regulations?

Yes. Every opt-in and opt-out is logged. All conversations stored. TCPA compliance built in from the start, not an afterthought.
